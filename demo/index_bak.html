Welcome to SDAPS Creator.

Support Users in Creating a LaTeX Questionnaire that can printed, scanned and processed with SDAPS. See http://www.sdaps.org for further details about SDAPS.

SDAPS Optical Markup Recognition

SDAPS is an Optical SDA Optical Mark Recognition (OMR) program, that allows to use printed Paper and Pencil Questionnaires to be processed automatically with SDAPS and get a spreadsheet document of the filled results. The software was developed by Benjamin Berg and is written in python. If offers the processing of questionnaire created with LibreOffice and LaTeX. The questonnaires created in LaTeX works best for OMR. SDAPScreator is just a HTML/Javascript tool that runs locally on your Firefox browser to generate a LaTeX questionnaire. For processing those questionnaires you have to install SDAPS. You can download an image of SDAPS based on Linux, that can be install even on your old PC. The image is provided by the University of Koblenz-Landau can be downloaded at http://www.uni-landau.de/sdaps/ and installed on your PC or in VirtualBox if you want to install it on non-Linux Computer e.g. on WlND0WS or MacOSX in virtual maschine.
